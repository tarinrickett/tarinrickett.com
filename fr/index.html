Bungee

I wanted my site to have a personality to it, which is why I searched for decorative header font families first. Bungee had exactly what I was looking for, a hint of personality, while still maintaining readability and customization, granting me flexibility in my use.

I used the 'Shade' font, the most decorative, for my T icon. I used the 'Inline' font, which retains the style of Shade without being too busy, for my h1 headers. I adjusted the letter spacing to be larger to make certain that the headers were easily digestable to a reader.

Open Sans

Open Sans was a suggested compliment to Bungee headers. It makes a perfect pairing for longer text chunks like these paragraphs. It maintains a similar style to Bungee, rounded and sans-serif with very veritical orientation.

Inspiration

I've always been a fan of 'long-scrolly' websites. One theme I found while researching was 'Oxygen'. I was impressed by the cleanliness of their structure and the simplicity retained while incorporating transitions and animations to make the experience still feel engaging and interactive.
